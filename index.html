Welcome to WebSpeakerJs Example

This page demonstrates the WebSpeakerJs component, a simple tool to add text-to-speech functionality to your website. Just include the component in your HTML, specify the target text element, and enjoy the speech synthesis feature.

Below is an example text about space exploration. Click the speaker icon to hear it read aloud.

A Brief Overview of Space Exploration

Space exploration is an enduring human endeavor that seeks to understand the cosmos beyond the confines of our planet. From the first satellite, Sputnik, launched by the Soviet Union in 1957, to the first moon landing by Apollo 11 in 1969, humanity has continually pushed the boundaries of space knowledge and travel.
